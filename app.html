📌 Important Tips

💡

Tier I cutoff usually 130–145 for General category. Aim for 160+ to be safe.

⚡

GA section is the easiest scoring area. Invest time in Current Affairs (last 6 months).

📊

Quantitative Aptitude: Focus on Arithmetic (40% questions) and Geometry/Trigonometry (30%).

🔑

Reasoning: Non-verbal (Figure series, Cubes) and Puzzles are high-weight topics in Tier I.
